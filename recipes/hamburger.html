Best Hamburger Ever

Description

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Make patties with ground beef and onions
  2. Heat large saucepan and fry 5 minutes per side
  3. Assemble cooked burger on bun
